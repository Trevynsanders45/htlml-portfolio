The Best Movies According To Trevyn

My Top Three Movies

The Butterfly Effect

What really captivated me was the film's exploration of how tiny changes in the past can lead to massive, often unintended consequences in the future. The whole concept of being able to alter your life by revisiting memories was both fascinating and terrifying. It really made me think about how every little decision we make can ripple out in unexpected ways.

Happy Gilmore

What I love most about this film is its perfect blend of ridiculous humor and heart. The scenes where Happy tries to adapt his aggressive hockey style to the refined world of golf are absolutely hilarious. I mean, who can forget the epic showdown with Shooter McGavin or the iconic fight with Bob Barker? Those moments are pure comedy gold.
